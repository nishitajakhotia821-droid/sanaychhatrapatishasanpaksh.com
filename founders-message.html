Founder's Message

Vision, leadership, and the foundation of Sanay Chatrapati Shasan Paksh

```

Dear Citizens,

Sanay Chatrapati Shasan paksh is more than a political party—it is a movement inspired by the ideals of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj and Rajmata Jijau, dedicated to justice, empowerment, and inclusive progress. Our vision is to build a nation where every citizen is respected, every voice is heard, and every community has the opportunity to prosper.

The foundation of this movement was laid through a shared commitment to public service and nation-building. Before the formation of the party, Prof. Namdev Jadhav represented India’s spiritual and philosophical heritage on the global stage, becoming only the second Hindu philosopher after Swami Vivekananda to address the Parliament of the World's Religions in Chicago on 15 August 2023.

What began as a discussion between Prof. Namdev Jadhav and Mr. Pius Pullikottil in June 2024 soon evolved into a vision for transformative governance. That vision became reality with the official registration of Sanay Chatrapati Shasan on 25 October 2024.

Today, we invite every citizen to join us in creating a stronger, more prosperous, and united India. Together, through integrity, service, and collective action, we can build a future rooted in opportunity, equality, and national progress.

With respect and commitment,

Prof. Namdev Jadhav
Co-Founder, Sanay Chatrapati Shasan

Mr. Pius Pullikottil
Co-Founder, Sanay Chatrapati Shasan

Mr. Rupesh Hemant Gugale
Co-Founder, Sanay Chatrapati Shasan
```
