Our Leadership

Dedicated leaders committed to nation building, public service and people-centric governance.

```

श्री. पायस वर्गीस

संस्थापक राष्ट्रीय सचिव

  • Post-Graduate in Political Science and Law with an MBA in Personnel Management.
  • Veteran trade union leader and labour law practitioner.
  • Actively involved in workers' rights movements and civil-political engagement since 1991.
  • Brings extensive experience in grassroots activism, social justice and inclusive governance.
```
```

प्राध्यापक नामदेवराव जाधव

राष्ट्रीय पक्षप्रमुख

  • Renowned historian, author and motivational speaker.
  • Author of more than 45 books including Shivaji: The Management Guru.
  • Widely recognized for promoting the leadership philosophy of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj.
  • In 2023 became the second Indian philosopher after Swami Vivekananda to address the Parliament of the World's Religions in Chicago.
```

श्री रुपेशशेठ हेमंतजी गुगळे पाटील (जैन)

राष्ट्रीय कार्यकारी अध्यक्ष

  • Entrepreneur and social leader dedicated to community development and public service.
  • Focused on youth empowerment, organizational growth and people-centric initiatives.
  • Plays a key role in shaping the party's outreach and strengthening grassroots connections.
  • Advocates practical solutions, transparent leadership and sustainable development.
